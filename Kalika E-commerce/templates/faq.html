FAQs

1. General Questions

Q1: What is Kalika Industrial eCommerce?

A: Kalika Industrial eCommerce is an e-commerce platform providing a wide range of industrial supplies, including MRO products, safety equipment, power tools, and more.

Q2: Where is Kalika Industrial eCommerce based?

A: Kalika Industrial eCommerce operates from Ahmednagar MIDC, Maharashtra, India, but caters to customers nationwide through our online platform.

Q3: Do I need an account to place an order?

A: While you can browse products without an account, creating an account allows you to track orders, save preferences, and receive exclusive offers.

2. Products and Orders

Q4: How can I search for products on your website?

A: Use the search bar at the top of our homepage or browse through the categories listed on our site to find specific products.

Q5: What should I do if I can't find a product?

A: Contact us at info@kalikaindia.com with the details of the product you're looking for, and we’ll assist you.

Q6: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

A: Orders can only be modified or canceled before they are processed. Contact our support team immediately for assistance.

3. Payments and Billing

Q7: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and other secure online payment options.

Q8: Are my payment details secure?

A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your data.

Q9: Can I get an invoice for my order?

A: Yes, an invoice will be emailed to you after your order is confirmed. You can also download it from your account dashboard.

4. Shipping and Delivery

Q10: Where do you deliver?

A: We currently deliver across India. For specific locations, please check during checkout.

Q11: How long does delivery take?

A: Delivery times vary by location, but orders are usually delivered within 3–7 business days.

Q12: What are the shipping charges?

A: Shipping charges, if any, are displayed during checkout. Free shipping may be available on select orders or promotions.

5. Returns and Refunds

Q13: What is your return policy?

A: Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery, provided they are unused, in original packaging, and meet our return policy terms.

Q14: How do I initiate a return or refund?

A: Contact our support team at info@kalikaindia.com with your order details to begin the process.

Q15: When will I receive my refund?

A: Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after the returned product is received and inspected.
