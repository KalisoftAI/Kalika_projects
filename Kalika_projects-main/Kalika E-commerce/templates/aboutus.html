About Us

Welcome to Kalika Enterprises

Established in 1990 in the Ahmednagar MIDC area, Kalika Enterprises was founded with the vision of fulfilling industrial needs by providing a wide range of quality products delivered at the right time. Our mission is to contribute to the success of industries by offering reliable and efficient solutions tailored to their specific requirements.

In response to evolving market trends and increasing demand from industries across India, we proudly operate as Kalika E-Commerce, a 24x7 service platform. Guided by our core principle, "You Choose, Demand, & We Provide," we ensure a seamless and satisfying customer experience.

At Kalika Enterprises, we go beyond delivering products; we deliver value and build long-lasting relationships with our customers. Our commitment to exceptional customer service, diverse product offerings, and timely delivery has earned us the trust and loyalty of clients nationwide.

We specialize in procuring industrial supplies, including MRO products, safety equipment, electrical components, lighting solutions, cleaning and housekeeping essentials, power tools, and a wide range of other industry-critical items. Our online platform is a one-stop solution, offering a curated selection of high-quality utility products designed to meet diverse industrial requirements.

Together, let's achieve your goals and drive industrial excellence with Kalika Enterprises.

Our team at Kalika Enterprises believes in continuous improvement and innovation. We actively listen to our clients' needs and leverage cutting-edge technologies to enhance our services. Thank you for choosing us as your trusted partner in industrial excellence.
